Contribute to TUNILip+

Record 13 short videos to help us build the largest Tunisian dialect lip reading dataset. Your contribution directly improves the model's accuracy for the deaf and hard-of-hearing community.

📋 Recording Guidelines

  • Use your phone's back camera for best quality
  • Position yourself 60–100 cm from the camera
  • Ensure your face is well-lit and facing the camera directly
  • Each video should be 4–5 seconds and contain one word only
  • Record in a quiet place with a neutral background
  • Speak at a natural pace — no need to over-pronounce
